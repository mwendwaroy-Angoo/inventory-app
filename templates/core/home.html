About Us

Duka Mwecheche is a Kenyan-built platform designed specifically for micro, small and medium enterprises. We understand the hustle of running a business — tracking stock on paper, missing sales, losing money to bad records.

Our mission is simple: give every duka the digital tools that big retailers use — inventory management, an online storefront, mobile payments, and real-time analytics — at zero cost.
